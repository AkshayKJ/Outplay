About The Dream

The Vision

The Dream is a website aimed to bring the gamers in India closer. Our aim is to organise and promote gaming in every way possible and open up this field for the gamers as a serious professional career. We Indians have the potential to rule the gaming world and The Dream's aim is to make this happen. Our team is full of gaming enthusiasts and we know how well gaming can turn up so we want to bring out the best of the gamers outta their homes and provide them a platform to perform on a higher level and to be proud of their passion and follow it with no second thoughts.
